INTRO

I determined to explore through my poems what happens to the self and the soul during and after seemingly unspeakable violence and how, successfully or not, we make language out of it.

ABOUT

I spent a lifetime inside the destruction.

And like anyone, I made a world someplace else.

OIL

I am using pop references, but not in a light or gimmicky way. The poems are exploring and troubling something. My references may look different from someone else's, but in my life I experience the Real Housewives more than I experience Greek myth. These are my contemporary myths and symbols.

SURVEILLANCE

Experiment.

Write to failure. Do not stop at success, or snaps or personal affirmation. Write until you are asking yourself, is this any good? If you surprise yourself, you just might surprise an audience or reader.
