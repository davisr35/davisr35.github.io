Audre Lorde
I was going to die, sooner or later, whether or not I had even spoken myself.
My silences had not protected me. Your silences will not protect you....
I began to ask each time: "What's the worst that could happen to me if I tell this truth?"
Push yourself a little further than you dare.
And the speaking will get easier and easier. And you will find you have fallen in love with your own vision,
which you may never have realized you had. And you will lose some friends and lovers, and realize you don't miss them...
new ones will find you and cherish you...
And at last you'll know with surpassing certainty that only one thing is more frightening than speaking your truth.
And that is not speaking.
